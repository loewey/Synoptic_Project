Head Pain

Content specific to head pain.

Mild Concussion

- Rest

- Avoid stress

- Hold Ice to affected area

Deep Cut

Hold clean cloth to wound until bleeding stops

visit Doctor

lift wound above heart area

Fever

Drink water

Apply warm damp cloth to forehead

lie down

eat fruits such as oranges and lemons

Eye Infection

apply cold cloth to the eye

clean with salt water

drink warm water and honey

apply wet tea bag over the eye
