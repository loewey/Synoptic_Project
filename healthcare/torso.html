Torso Pain

Content specific to torso pain.

Back Pain

- gentle Stretches

- apply hot/cold patches on area

- massage area and stretch

Shortness of breath

- Take slow even breaths

- Avoid Smoke and burning rubbish

- When breathing out put lips together as if blowing a candle

Stomach ache

- Drink lots of water

- Have some ginger

- Have some tea (Chamomile or peppermint)

- Avoid laying down

- Eat foods such as rice and bananas

Chest pain

- Description of solution 2

- Eat almonds

- Apply cold packs to the chest

- Eat foods like ginger and garlic
